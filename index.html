Small Bodies Image Browser
The Small Bodies Image Browser (SBIB) is designed to graphically search through the large number of images quickly. It takes about 2-5 minutes to download the database to your browser depending on your internet connection. Once done, data searches are very fast. Currently SBIB accesses OSIRIS-REX Bennu, New Horizons Pluto Encounter, Dawn Vesta, Dawn Ceres, Near Eros, and Cassini Rhea, Iapetus and Enceladus data. Additional missions and objects will be added as development continues.

SBIB will display a map and options to search for images.

Key Features (Details)

  • Search by coordinates
  • Show an entire sequence
  • Display image data
  • Draw image footprint on the map
  • Show thumbnail images, either map projected or original
  • Download raw or processed images

  • Pluto's about page

  • Ceres' about page

    Available data including extended mission

    • Framing Camera

    • Visible and InfraRed Mapping Spectrometer

  • Vesta's about page

    Available data

    • Framing Camera

    • Visible and InfraRed Mapping Spectrometer
      • Level 1B (excluding approach & departure)

NEAR Shoemaker Mission to 433 Eros


  • Eros' about page

    Available data

    • Multi-Spectral Imager

Cassini Mission: Rhea (Saturn V)


  • Rhea's about page
